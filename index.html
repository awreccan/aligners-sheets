Set up sync

“22” stores your wear log in your own private Google Sheet so your phone, Siri, and the app all stay in sync — the Sheet is the database, and you can open it and fix the log by hand anytime. No code, no setup — just paste your Sheet link.

  1. In Safari, go to sheets.new — it makes a blank Sheet. (If a “Open in the app?” banner pops up, just tap ✕ / “Continue in browser”. No app needed.)
  2. Tap Share (top-right) → under General access tap “Restricted” and change it to “Anyone with the link” → set the role to Editor → Copy link → Done. (why Editor? — so the app can write your wear log into it.)
  3. Come back here and paste the copied Sheet link:

Your Sheet link is stored only on this device. Anyone who has the link can edit that Sheet, so keep it private — like a password.
